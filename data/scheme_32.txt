Description:
Demonstration for popularizing new crops/ varieties, and new
technologies in Agriculture and allied sectors. Diversification of
production systems like Dryland Horticulture, Agro Forestry,
Household production system,Adoption of proven technology,
activities like INM, IPM, Fodder development, Organic farming,
Multiple cropping, Improved inter cropping seed village concept,
Fisheries development in village ponds, farm ponds and tanks,
Sericulture, back yard poultry,Livestock management and other
micro enterprises etc. are carried out under this component.
